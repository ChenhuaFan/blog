# 连读导致的听不懂

  • That's right, no traveling involved. I wouldn't expect volunteers to travel to a site, especially in the middle of the semester.

  • I'm hoping to get five or six. I've asked for volunteers i***n a***ll of the classes I teach, but no one has responded.

    这个句子中，I've asked for … ‘ve 发 [v]的音，与 ask 的 a 连读 [væsk] 之后 ked 被省略 直接是 vas for. one has 基本是 one's responded.

  • Is there ... is there any way I can use the experience to get some extra credit in class? I mean, can I write a paper about it?

    这里 some extra 存在一个连读。some 最后的 [m] 会和 ex 的 [ɛkstrə] 连读。

  • And then I'll schedule a training class next week at a time that's convenient for everyone.

    经典 I'll 连读。值得注意，尤其是前面一个 then 会和 I 连读。

# 句型或单词不熟悉导致的听不懂

  • Hi, Professor Archer, you know how in class last week you said that you were looking for students who were interested in volunteering for your archaeology project?

    这里也存在一个 for your 的弱读，需要注意一下。同时 how 在此处无实意，只是引导作用。

    ***意：***嗨，阿彻教授，你知道上周在课堂上你是怎么说的吗？你正在寻找对于考古项目志愿者感兴趣的学生。

  • This used to be an agricultural area and we already know that where the main lecture hall now stands, there once were farm house and barn that were erected in the late 1700s.

    ***意：***现在是大讲堂的这里曾经是农耕区，这里原来有茅屋和谷仓

    同时注意：an agriculture 存在连读。

  • We're excavating near the lecture hall to see what types of artifacts we find, you know, things people used in the past that got buried when the campus was constructed.

    things 做补充说明，people … past 做后置定语；that got … constructed 做定语从句

    同时注意 that 的读法，很弱。

  • A senior researcher, I think you know John Franklin, my assistant, is on site every day.

    高级研究员

# 弱读 & 略读导致的听不懂

  • I think it'll depend on what type of work you do in the excavation, but I imagine we can arrange something.

    这里是一个经典的 it’ll 连读。需要注意。同时在其他的练习中，还存在 you'll we'll 等。

  • Ok. I'll wait to hear from you. Thanks a lot for accepting me

    这也太快了。"我会等你的消息。"

# 疑难杂症

  • Oh, good, that's a relief. Actually, that's why I'm volunteering for the project - to get experience. What kind of work is it?

    在听写文本时，没有加上冠词。需要重新看看如何使用冠词了

单词 & 短语 释义 语音
arrange 安排，商量
site 考古学地点，挖掘地点
