# 连读导致的听不懂

  • OK, so we've got a spectrum of a beam of sunlight and it looks like the colors bleed into each other, uh, there’re no interruptions, just a band flowing from violet to green to yellow to…you get the idea.

    经典的连读。m-o；f-a

  • Well what happen**s if the sunlight spectrum i**s magnified? Maybe you all didn't do the reading.

# 句型或单词不熟悉导致的听不懂

  • Now astronomy didn't really, uh, bloom into the science it is today until the development of spectroscopy.

    **意：**如今，直到光谱学发展起来了天文学才成为真正的科学。

  • Spectroscopy is basically the study of spectra and spectral lines of light, and specifically for us, the light from stars.

    For us 的连读，应该注意一下咯。

  • Before we get into that, though, it’s probably a good thing to back up a bit.

    意：在我们讨论这个之前，我们最好是回顾一下之前学过的。

    同时，存在很多连读现象。

  • The light that we see with our human eyes as a band of rainbow color falls in the range of what's called visible light. 💡

    中间 as … range: 集合成一系列可见光。

    Falls in the range 落在一个范围内

  • Anyone wanna take a stab at the scientific term for visible light…

    有同学想尝试说一些可见光的科学术语吗？

    Take a stab at：做个尝试

    {% asset_img takeastabat.png take a stab at %}

  • Each pattern stands for a distinct chemical element, and so different sets or patterns of spectral lines mean that the star has a different chemical composition.

    注意 element 的连读。

  • Well, a kind of spectroscopic library of elements was compiled using flame tests.

    注意 element 的连读。

  • This light is passed through a prism, which breaks it up into a spectrum, and a unique pattern, kind of like a chemical fingerprint, of spectral lines for that element appears.

    一个插入语，就看不懂了。

  • There was a pretty extensive library of spectral line patterns of elements even by the 1860’s.

    Library of spectral 服了。

  • Well here's what you’d see:I want you to notice that this spectru**m is in**terrupted by dark lines, called spectral lines.

  • So, how do we know which spectral patterns match up with which elements?

    读这么快，谁能顶得住啊。

  • A know**n element, uh, say a piece of iron for example, is heated in a** pure gas flame. The iron eventually heats to the point that it radiates light.

    element 连读；iron 铁啊，兄弟。is 被听成 it‘s；heated in a 存在连读，in 没听出来导致不明白什么意思；

    that it 存在连读，没听到 it；radiate 作动词辐射，没反应出来。
