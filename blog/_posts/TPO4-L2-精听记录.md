  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74963_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

* You'll be reading one of Ralph Waldo Emerson's best-known essays, Self-Reliance, and comparing it with **<u>his poems *and*</u>** other works.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74964_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

  And 听不出来。[n]

* I think this essay has the potential to be quite meaningful for all of you—as young people who probably wonder about things like truth, <u>**and where your lives are going**</u> … all <u>***sorts of***</u> profound questions.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74965_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

* <i class="fa fa-star" aria-hidden="true" style="color:orange"></i> Knowing something about Emerson's philosophies will help you when you read Self-Reliance. And basically, <u>one of the main belief**s that he** had</u>, was about truth.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74967_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

  H 击穿不发音，he [i] belief<u>s t</u>ha<u>t ~~h~~e</u>

* <i class="fa fa-star" aria-hidden="true" style="color:orange"></i> Not <u>**that it's**</u> something that we can be <u>***taught***</u>... Emerson **<u>says it's</u>** found within ourselves.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74969_20181115113507.mp3" id="its" controls></audio>

  Tha<u>t i</u>**t's**[z]；Say<u>s i</u>**t's**[z]

  **大意：**这并不是说我们可以学会……爱默生说这是在我们自己身上发现的。

* <i class="fa fa-star" aria-hidden="true" style="color:orange"></i> So this truth... the idea that it's <u>**in**</u> each one of us... is one of the first point<u>**s that you'll see**</u> Emerson making in thi<u>**s e**</u>ssay.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74971_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

  In ~~th~~is e 似乎没有发 th 的音。point**s th**at 没有连读。需要注意

* <i class="fa fa-star" aria-hidden="true" style="color:orange"></i> Most of us <u>**aren't**</u> in touch <u>with</u> <u>**ourselves**</u>, in a way, so we just <u>**aren't**</u> <u>***capable of***</u> recognizing profound truths.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74976_20181115113507.mp3" id="arent" controls></audio>

  Aren't 否定

* **<u>You'll</u>** see <u>that he</u> writes about... well, first, conformity.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74980_20181115113507.mp3" id="youll" controls></audio>

  You 和 you'll 分不清；tha<u>t ~~h~~e</u> [t-i]

## 句型或单词不熟悉导致的听不懂

* It's a bit abstract, but he's **very** into, ah, into each person <u>believing his or her own thought</u>.

  大意：这有点抽象，但是他就在，啊，就在每个人的身边，那些相信自己想法的人身上。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74972_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

  Believing … 后置定语 person；very 强调，

  > very into 就在那儿. ——文学少女

* <i class="fa fa-star" aria-hidden="true" style="color:orange"></i> he ties tha<u>**t i**</u>n **with** <u>a sort of</u> universal truth

  大意：艾默生把自己的想法与一种“普遍真理”联系起来。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74975_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

  这里很明显，s 和 th 没有连读；此外，with 的略读真的很不明显。<u>***需要多听***</u>

* <i class="fa fa-star" aria-hidden="true" style="color:orange"></i> So, Emerson is really <u>**into**</u> each <u>**individual**</u> <u>believing in, and trusting, **him or herself**</u>.

  大意：所以艾默生对那些相信并信任自己的人很感兴趣。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74979_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

  Into prep. 对…感兴趣；believing in … 做后置定语(individual) 听的时候要注意句子结构。

* <i class="fa fa-star" aria-hidden="true" style="color:orange"></i> He criticizes <u>**the people of his time**</u>, <u>for abandoning</u> their own minds and their own wills <u>**for the sake of**</u> conformity and consistency.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74981_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

  大意：他批判跟他<u>**同时代**</u>的人，那些为了一致性和一贯性而抛弃了自己的想法和愿望人们。

  The people of his time 不太能 get 到。

* They try to fit in with the rest of the world, even though <u>**it's at odds with their**</u> beliefs and their identities.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74982_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

  同时注意，存在连读 it's at odd；同时 with 弱读

* Therefore, <u>**it's**</u> best to be a **nonconformist**—to do your own thing, not worrying about what other people think.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74983_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

* <u><i class="fa fa-star" aria-hidden="true" style="color:orange"></i> That's an important point</u>—he really <u>**drives this argument home**</u> throughout the essay.

  大意：这点是非常重要的，艾默生确实通过自己的文章**阐明**了这一点。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74984_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

* When you're reading I want you to think about that, and why that kind of thought would be relevant to the reader<u>**s of his time**</u>.

  大意：当你们读这篇文章的时候，我要你们思考这一点，以及这种想法为什么会和他同时代的读者相关。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74986_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

* Remember, this is 1838. Self-reliance was a novel idea at the time, and United States citizens were <u>less secure about themselves as individuals and as Americans.</u>

  大意：记住，在1838 年。“自立”是一种新颖的想法，而美国公民作为拥有美国国籍的个体不是很认同自我。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74987_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

* Emerson wanted to give people something to really think about. Help them find their <u>**own**</u> way and ah, <u>what **it meant** to be who they were</u>.

  大意：艾默生想给人们提供可以付之思忖的东西。帮他们找到自己的处世途径和自我认识。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74991_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

  What it meant to be who they were.

* So, that's something that I think is definitely as relevant today as it was then... probably, uh... especially among young adults like yourselves.

  大意：这一点就是我所认为的以前跟现在都通用的相关性，嗯，也许，特别是像你们这样的成年人中。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74994_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

* You know, uh, college being a time to sort of really think about who you are and where you're going.

  大意：你知道的，大学的时候，你们就必须考虑自己的定位和发展方向。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74995_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>

* As a way <u>**to sort of**</u> not lose your own self and identity in the world? To have your own truth and not be afraid to listen to it?

  大意：作为<u>**一种**</u>不失去自我和在这个世界上的定位的方式。认识到自己的真我，不要害怕去倾听灵魂的呼唤。

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/1848/74998_20181115113507.mp3" controls></audio>
