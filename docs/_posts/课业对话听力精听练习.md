#### 标记说明: 

* <u>下划线</u>：至少不是初次听出，但直到检查时已经改正

* **黑体加粗**：单词不认识

* **<u>黑体加粗下划线</u>**：存在连读

* ***<u>黑体加粗下划线倾斜</u>***：直到检查时，漏听&听错部分



## 语音问题

* Right, 3 and 4, well, I **didn't** get something you said on class Monday.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/3748/27961_20180706105357.mp3" controls></audio>

  * 这里的 didn't 没有听到。影响了句子理解

* Alright? Do you remember wha<u>t I~~t~~ was</u> about?

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/3748/27963_20180706105357.mp3" controls></audio>

  * What it was about？存在连读

* Right, so health clubs offer high quality facilities, and also they sell an image about people having more fun, relating better to others and improving their **own lives** if they become members.

  <audio src="https://img.kmf.com/qmmp3/3748/27991_20180706105357.mp3" controls></audio>

  * Own lives 存在连读

## 词汇 & 句子 & 语法问题
